## Overview

This compliance matrix provides a jurisdiction-by-jurisdiction summary of the legal and regulatory requirements relevant to the Signet Protocol. It covers data protection, consent ages, breach notification, cross-border transfer rules, child protection, electronic signature frameworks, and key professional bodies that serve as trust anchors for Tier 3 and Tier 4 verifications.

**Important:** This matrix is a reference guide, not legal advice. Laws and regulations change frequently. Always verify current requirements with qualified legal counsel in the relevant jurisdiction.
